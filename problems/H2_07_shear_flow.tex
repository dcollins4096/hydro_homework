
   \pts{6} Plane Parallel Couette Flow:  Given fluid between planes, infinite in
    $\hat{x}$ and $\hat{z}$, 
    one fixed and one moving with a velocity $V \hat{x}$, separated by a
    distance $d$ (in the $\hat{y}$ direction),
    find all three velocity components.

      \solution{
      \emph{Solution}
  
      \begin{align}
        \dbd{v}{t} &= 0\\
        v_y &= 0\\
        \dbd{}{x} &= 0
      \end{align}
      So N.S. becomes
      \begin{align}
        0 &= \mu \frac{\partial^2 v_x}{\partial y^2}\\
        \dbd{v_x}{y} &= C\\
        v_x &= C y + B\\
        v_x(y=0) &= 0 \\
        v_x(y=d) &= V \\
        v_x(y) &= \frac{V}{d} y\\
      \end{align}
      }
